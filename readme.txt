Ni One Page Inventory Management System For WooCommerce provides the option to purchase and maintained the stock for different location.

== Description ==

<h3>Purchase Order System</h3>
<p>
Add purchase order to increase the WooCommerce product (simple or variation) stock quantity, purchase order maintain the <strong>stock location</strong>, <strong>purchase supplier</strong>, <strong>purchase date</strong> and <strong>purchase number</strong>. Inventory plug-in provide the option to view the previous purchase product history.
</p>
<h3>Previous Purchase Price History</h3> 
<p>
Previous purchase price is very important when you purchase the product next time, One page inventory plug-in maintain the <strong>previous purchase price</strong> of all products with purchase date and purchase supplier. 
</p>
<h3>Multi-location Purchase Order</h3>
<p>One page inventory plug-in provides the option to make the purchase order from different location, create <strong>multiple locations</strong> and make the purchase order base on location. </p>
<h2>Online Location Stock</h2>
<p>Inventory plug-in provide the option to set the location for <strong>online sales</strong> create location, go to setting and set the location for online sales. All product stock reduce from <strong>online location</strong>.</p>
<h3>Inventory Management</h3>
<p>Represent the all product(simple or variation) in <strong>grid</strong> or <strong>tabular</strong> format, so it easy to maintain stock quantity  of any product, the plug-in provide the option to increase or decrease the stock quantity without going to individual product.  </p>
<h3>Manage product</h3>
<p>Mange product easy because all product is show in the grid or tabular format and itâ€™s provide the option to change the product price, product stock, product name and <strong>product SKU</strong>.</p>
<h3>Search Product</h3>
</p>Search product is difficult task if you have more product but we provide the option where you can search the product <strong>alphabetically</strong>, product name, <strong>product SKU</strong> and product status.</p>


<h4>Key Feature of Ni One Page Inventory Management System for WooCommerce  </h4>
<ul>

</ul>

== Installation ==

1. Use the WordPress plugin installer or upload files to WordPress plugin directory.
2. Activate "Ni One Page Inventory Management System for WooCommerce" plugin from admin panel.
3. Find "Ni Inventory" menu in admin.
